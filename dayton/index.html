Signs You Need Emergency Plumbing in Dayton

  • Water meter spinning when no fixtures are in use
  • Bubbling toilet when using other plumbing fixtures
  • Persistent sewage odors inside or around your home
  • Visible water stains on ceilings, walls or basement floors
  • Unusual sounds in your plumbing system (hammering, whistling)
  • Reduced water pressure throughout multiple fixtures
  • White chalky residue on fixtures (hard water buildup)
  • No hot water during Dayton's cold winter months
  • Unexplained increase in water bills (common in older Dayton homes)
  • Pooling water in basement or crawl spaces after rain
