Signs You Need Emergency Plumbing in Houston

  • Water meter spinning when no fixtures are in use
  • Bubbling toilet when using other plumbing fixtures
  • Persistent sewage odors inside or around your home
  • Visible water stains on ceilings, walls or floors
  • Unusual sounds in your plumbing system (hammering, whistling)
  • Reduced water pressure throughout multiple fixtures
  • Backed-up sinks or tubs that won't drain properly
  • Water pooling near foundation (common Houston issue)
  • Unexplained increase in water bills (potential slab leak)
